Bishop Joseph Mwangi Njekehu

Founder & Presiding Bishop

Biography

Bishop Joseph Mwangi Njekehu is the Founder and Presiding Bishop of House of Prayer Worshippers Ministries. He is a dedicated servant of God with a strong calling in teaching, discipleship, leadership development, and ministerial training.

He has devoted many years to theological study, ministry training, church leadership, and raising believers who are grounded in prayer, holiness, and the Word of God.

Through his leadership, House of Prayer Worshippers Church has grown into a vibrant ministry with multiple branches across Kenya, impacting lives through evangelism, discipleship, worship, and community outreach.
