Bishop Joseph Mwangi Njekehu

Founder & Presiding Bishop

Biography

Bishop Joseph Mwangi Njekehu is the Founder and Presiding Bishop of House of Prayer Worshippers Church. He is a dedicated servant of God with a strong calling in teaching, discipleship and leadership development.

For more than 13 years he has devoted himself to theological study, ministry training, evangelism, discipleship and church leadership. His ministry focuses on raising a generation of genuine saints rooted in prayer, holiness and the Word of God.
