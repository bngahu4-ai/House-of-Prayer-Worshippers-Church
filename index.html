WHO WE ARE

About House of Prayer Worshippers Church

House of Prayer Worshippers Church is a Christ -centered ministry committed to raising believers who live holy, authentic, and Spirit-led lives. We are a growing family of faith passionate about worship, prayer, discipleship, and the teaching of God's Word.

OUR JOURNEY

Our History

Founded over 10 years ago by Bishop Joseph Mwangi Njekehu, House of Prayer Worshippers Church was established with a vision to build a strong spiritual foundation in the lives of believers and to raise a generation rooted in faith, truth, and holiness.

What began as a humble ministry has grown into a vibrant church with multiple branches across Kenya, impacting lives through biblical teaching, fervent prayer, worship, evangelism, and community outreach.

Our headquarters is located in Kaharo, Kirinyaga County, along the Kagumo–Baricho Road, serving as the heart of a ministry dedicated to spreading the Gospel and transforming lives through the power of Jesus Christ.

Meet Our Founder & Presiding Bishop

Bishop Joseph Mwangi Njekehu

Founder & Presiding Bishop

Bishop Joseph Mwangi Njekehu is the Founder and Presiding Bishop of House of Prayer Worshippers Ministries. He is a dedicated servant of God with a strong calling in biblical teaching, discipleship, leadership development, and ministerial training.

Through years of ministry, theological training, and church leadership, he has remained committed to raising believers who are rooted in prayer, holiness, sound doctrine, and the transforming power of God's Word.
